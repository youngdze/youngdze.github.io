浏览器缓存

  • Expires: Fri, 11 Aug 2017, 17:00:00 GMT
  • Last-Modified: Fri, 11 Aug 2017, 17:00:00 GMT
  • If-Modified-Since: Fri, 11 Aug 2017, 17:00:00 GMT

  • If-None-Match: “v2.6”
